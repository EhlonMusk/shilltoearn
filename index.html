HOW IT WORKS

A fully automated system to track and verify your $SHILL tweets

1

Join the Leaderboard

Add your X username and Solana wallet address to join the leaderboard. We'll fetch your profile and begin monitoring your $SHILL activity. Both pieces of information are required for verification and prize distribution.

2

Tweet with $SHILL

Create tweets or replies containing the keyword "$SHILL". Each tweet becomes eligible for points. Any tweet format works as long as it contains "$SHILL".

3

Submit for Verification

Copy your tweet URL and submit it through your profile page. Our verification system kicks in automatically. Manual submission is required to prevent automated spam.

4

AI + Timeline Verification

Our system performs dual verification:

Timeline Check: Verifies the tweet exists in your recent activity

AI Content Analysis: Confirms the tweet contains "$SHILL"

Ownership Verification: Ensures the tweet is from your account

5

Earn Points & Compete

Each verified tweet earns you 1 point. Climb the leaderboard and compete for the Creator Fund! Prize distribution will be sent directly to your registered Solana wallet address.

6

Points Reset & New Cycle

After every distribution (every 15 minutes), all users' points automatically reset to 0. This ensures fair competition and gives everyone an equal chance to earn rewards in the next cycle.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does verification take?

Verification is instant! Results appear within seconds of submission.

Can I submit old tweets?

Yes, but they must appear in your recent timeline (last ~40 tweets/replies).

What if my tweet gets rejected?

The system will tell you exactly why - usually missing "$SHILL" or timeline visibility issues.

How is the Creator Fund distributed?

The top 5 shillers split the Creator Fund based on their final leaderboard positions.
